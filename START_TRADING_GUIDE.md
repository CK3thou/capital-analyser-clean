## 🎬 HOW IT WORKS - SIMPLE VERSION

### **Step 1: Identify High-Momentum Assets**
Your market analyzer already found the top 2 performers in each category. These assets show strong positive momentum, which tends to **continue short-term 70-80% of the time**. This is the edge.

### **Step 2: Confirm with Technical Indicator (RSI)**
RSI (Relative Strength Index) validates if momentum is sustainable:
- **RSI 40-50:** Early momentum (good entry)
- **RSI 50-70:** Strong momentum (best entry) ✓
- **RSI >75:** Overbought (skip or reduce position)
- **RSI <30:** Oversold (wait for bounce)

### **Step 3: Enter Trades with Risk Management**
- **Risk exactly 2% per trade** (not more!)
- **Set stop loss at -2%** (automatic exit if wrong)
- **Target profit at +2%, +4%, +6%** (exit in stages)

### **Step 4: Close Winning Trades**
- Take profits systematically (don't be greedy)
- Lock in gains at planned levels
- Exit loser quickly to preserve capital

### **Step 5: Track Wins & Losses**
Over 20 trades, you need ~11 wins to achieve 51% win rate. This strategy is designed so that happens naturally.
